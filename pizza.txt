

Recipe by me

Buy pizza base from super market
buy veggies such as red and green capcicum,paneer,chilli tomato,mushroom,onion .
Buy cheese,butter,chilli flakes,origano ,pizza sauces,etc


procedure

take the pizza base apply the pizza sauces on the base
cut the veggies and place the veggies on the base 
add cheese slices and greet butter and cheese add it on the top 
bake it in an oven at pre defined temprature

take out the pizza add chilli flakes and origano cut it into slices and serve it
